## 理念 Philosophy

本資料庫作為開放式世界觀框架公開。

This project is released as an open worldbuilding framework.

任何人都可以基於本框架創作自己的小說、角色、世界、遊戲、漫畫、動畫與商業作品。

People may create their own original stories, characters, worlds, games, comics, animations, and commercial works based on this framework.

他們自行創作出的原創角色、劇情、世界、設定擴展與作品屬於其創作者本人。

Their independently created original characters, plots, worlds, setting expansions, and works belong to their respective creators.

但是，Universe Wall Framework 的原始基底規則、官方術語及其既有定義、官方相容標準，仍屬於原作者的原始框架，不會因被他人使用而轉移。

However, the original base rules, official terminology and their established meanings, and official compatibility standard of the Universe Wall Framework remain part of the original creator’s framework and are not transferred by use.

## 官方相容規則 Official Compatibility Rule

Universe Wall Framework 的既有底層規則可以被擴展，但不能被覆寫。

The established base rules of the Universe Wall Framework may be expanded, but not overwritten.

作品不需要使用所有底層規則；但如果一部作品想宣稱自己「相容於官方 Universe Wall Framework」，就不能否定、矛盾或重新定義其作品中涉及或依賴的既有底層規則。

A work does not need to use every base rule. However, if it claims compatibility with the official Universe Wall Framework, it must not deny, contradict, or redefine any established base rule it touches or relies on.

可以新增規則、世界、種族、能力系統、歷史與解釋，只要它們是在既有基礎上擴展，而不是取代基礎。

New rules, worlds, species, systems, abilities, histories, and interpretations may be added, as long as they are built on top of the existing foundation rather than replacing it.

如果作品更動了底層規則，仍然可以自由發表，但應標示為：

**“Inspired by the Universe Wall Framework”**
而不是：

**“Compatible with the Universe Wall Framework.”**

Works that change the base rules may still be published, but they should be labeled as:

**“Inspired by the Universe Wall Framework”**
rather than:

**“Compatible with the Universe Wall Framework.”**

## 相容不等於官方正史 Compatibility Is Not Official Canon

「相容」僅表示該作品沒有否定、矛盾或重新定義其涉及或依賴的官方基底規則。

“Compatibility” only means that the work does not deny, contradict, or redefine the official base rules it touches or relies on.

「相容」不代表該作品屬於原作者故事作品的官方正史，也不代表原作者對該作品進行背書、贊助或正式認可。

“Compatibility” does not mean that the work is official canon of the original creator’s stories, nor does it mean the work is endorsed, sponsored, or formally approved by the original creator.

其他人基於本框架創作的作品，除非獲得原作者明確承認，否則皆屬於獨立作品，不屬於原作者故事作品的官方正史。

Works created by others based on this framework are independent works unless explicitly approved by the original creator. They are not part of the official canon of the original creator’s stories.

其他人基於本框架創作的作品中，其自行創作的角色、劇情、世界、設定擴展、遊戲、漫畫、動畫或其他原創內容，屬於其創作者本人。

Original characters, plots, worlds, setting expansions, games, comics, animations, or other original content independently created by others based on this framework belong to their respective creators.

在這些作品中使用到的 Universe Wall Framework 原始基底規則、官方術語及其既有定義、官方相容標準，仍歸屬於原作者的原始框架，不會因被他人使用而轉移。

The original base rules, official terminology and their established meanings, and official compatibility standard of the Universe Wall Framework used within such works remain part of the original creator’s framework and are not transferred by use.

任何人不得將 Universe Wall Framework 的原始基底規則宣稱為自己的原創框架。

No one may claim the original base rules of the Universe Wall Framework as their own original framework.

任何人不得將自己的衍生作品宣稱為原作者故事作品的官方正史，除非獲得原作者明確承認。

No one may claim their derivative works as official canon of the original creator’s stories unless explicitly approved by the original creator.

This interpretive authority applies only to the official base framework, official terminology definitions, and official compatibility standard of the Universe Wall Framework. It does not apply to independent original content created by others.

## 簡短原則 Short Principle

你可以在地基上建造自己的世界，但不能把地基挖掉後仍宣稱它與官方框架相容。

You may build your own world on top of the foundation, but you may not remove or replace the foundation and still claim compatibility with the official framework.
