# Universe Wall Framework

這是一個原創世界觀設定檔案庫，主要記錄「宇宙牆壁」、「虛」、「平行世界」、「平行鏈」、「有限宇宙」與「跨宇宙現象」等設定。

This is an original worldbuilding archive that mainly documents concepts such as Universe Walls, the Void, Parallel Worlds, Parallel Chains, Finite Universes, and cross-universe phenomena.

## 理念 Philosophy

本資料庫作為開放式世界觀框架公開。

This project is released as an open worldbuilding framework.

任何人都可以基於本框架創作自己的小說、角色、世界、遊戲、漫畫、動畫與商業作品。
他們創作出的原創角色、劇情、世界與作品屬於他們自己。

People may create their own original stories, characters, worlds, games, comics, animations, and commercial works based on this framework.
Their original characters, plots, worlds, and works belong to them.

## 官方相容規則 Official Compatibility Rule

Universe Wall Framework 

的既有底層規則可以被擴展，但不能被覆寫。

The established base rules of the Universe Wall Framework may be expanded, but not overwritten.

作品不需要使用所有底層規則；但如果一部作品想宣稱自己「相容於官方 Universe Wall Framework」，就不能否定、矛盾或重新定義其作品中涉及或依賴的既有底層規則。

A work does not need to use every base rule. However, if it claims compatibility with the official Universe Wall Framework, it must not deny, contradict, or redefine any established base rule it touches or relies on.

可以新增規則、世界、種族、能力系統、歷史與解釋，只要它們是在既有基礎上擴展，而不是取代基礎。

New rules, worlds, species, systems, abilities, histories, and interpretations may be added, as long as they are built on top of the existing foundation rather than replacing it.

如果作品更動了底層規則，仍然可以自由發表，但應標示為：
“Inspired by the Universe Wall Framework”
而不是：
“Compatible with the Universe Wall Framework.”

Works that change the base rules may still be published, but they should be labeled as:
“Inspired by the Universe Wall Framework”
rather than:
“Compatible with the Universe Wall Framework.”



其他人基於本框架創作的作品中，其自行創作的角色、劇情、世界、設定擴展、遊戲、漫畫、動畫或其他原創內容，屬於其創作者本人。

在這些作品中使用到的 Universe Wall Framework 原始基底規則、官方術語與官方相容標準，仍歸屬於原作者的原始框架，不會因被他人使用而轉移。

任何人不得將 Universe Wall Framework 的原始基底規則宣稱為自己的原創框架，也不得將自己的衍生作品宣稱為原作者故事作品的官方正史，除非獲得原作者明確承認。

Original characters, plots, worlds, setting expansions, games, comics, animations, or other original content created by others based on this framework belong to their respective creators.

The original base rules, official terminology, and official compatibility standard of the Universe Wall Framework used within such works remain part of the original creator’s framework and are not transferred by use.

No one may claim the original base rules of the Universe Wall Framework as their own original framework, nor may they claim their derivative works as official canon of the original creator’s stories unless explicitly approved by the original creator.
